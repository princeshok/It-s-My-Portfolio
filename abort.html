About Me

I was born on 18 May 2002 in Monrovia, Liberia. I'm a Liberian, studying programming as a Software Engineer. In Relation to my academic journey, I have participated in many academy activities. I graduated in 2020 from The Liberia Dujar High school with a High School Diploma and subsequently took and passed The West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) In Liberia. In 2023, I graduated from the YMCA Polytechnic college with a certificate in the Computer Literacy Training Program. My ambition of becoming a software Engineer developed during High School, which requires hard work and dedicating more time to it.
